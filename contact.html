FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What BMS protection is included?

All batteries include an integrated BMS with overcharge, over-discharge, overcurrent, short-circuit, and temperature protection monitored in real time.

Can batteries be connected in series or parallel?

Most 12V models support up to 4S4P configurations. The W-12-315H supports parallel only (max 4P) and W-36-100 parallel only (max 2P). Check the product spec for details.

How does the battery heater work?

On "H" models, the integrated heater activates automatically when the battery temperature drops below 0°C during charging, and stops once the battery reaches 10°C. It does not activate during discharge.

What charger should I use?

Use a dedicated LiFePO4 charger with constant current / constant voltage (CC/CV) and auto shut-off. Avoid trickle or float chargers. For 12V batteries, a charger set to 14.6V is correct.

Are the inverters compatible with my batteries?

Yes. All off-grid and hybrid inverters are compatible with both LiFePO4 and lead-acid 12V batteries. The H-12-3000W also supports lithium mode (14.4V charge voltage).

What warranty do the products carry?

All LiFePO4 batteries carry a 6-year manufacturer's warranty with an expected service life of 10 years and over 6000 charge cycles at 80% DoD.
