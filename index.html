About NordicWatt

Norwegian Distributor of Premium Energy Solutions

NordicWatt brings high-performance LiFePO4 battery technology and pure sine wave inverters to the Norwegian market. Whether you're outfitting a motorhome, a fishing boat, a cabin off the grid, or a solar energy system, we have the right solution.

All our products feature integrated Battery Management Systems (BMS), Bluetooth monitoring, and IP67 protection, backed by a 6-year manufacturer's warranty.

  • LiFePO4 lithium iron phosphate — the safest lithium chemistry
  • Integrated BMS with overcharge, over-discharge & overcurrent protection
  • Bluetooth 4.0 app monitoring for up to 8 batteries simultaneously
  • IP67 rated — fully waterproof for marine and outdoor use
  • Cold-weather heaters on selected models for Nordic conditions
  • 6-year manufacturer's warranty on all batteries
